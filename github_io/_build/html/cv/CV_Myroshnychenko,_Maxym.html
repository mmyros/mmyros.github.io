Research projects

Closed-loop excitation of hippocampal terminals from prefrontal cortex

Cortico-hippocampal interactions in the radial arm maze

  • Characterizing the process of planning in delayed spatial win-shift task using optogenetic inhibition of HC; shank and wire mPFC recordings

Effects of ethanol on interactions beetween VTA GABA and DA neurons and mPFC

  • Dissecting local and distal dynamic connectivity of ventral tegmental area using dual-site single-unit recordings, optogenetic stimulation, and pharmacological manipulations as a part of France-USA computational modeling collaboration
